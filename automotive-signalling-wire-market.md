<p><p>The automotive signaling wire market encompasses various types essential for vehicle communication and signaling functions. Main automotive signaling wires serve as the primary connections for power distribution. Front automotive signaling wires are used for headlights and indicators. Control automotive signaling wires facilitate interior function management, while floor, roof, and door wires connect to respective section signaling needs. Trunk lid wires manage rear signals. FPAS and RPAS wires relate to front and rear parking assist systems, while battery automotive signaling wires handle power supply tasks.</p></p>
