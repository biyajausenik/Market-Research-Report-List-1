<p><p>Rail wheel sensors are crucial for monitoring the health and performance of railway systems. The market is primarily divided into single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors focus on individual wheel tracking, providing precise data on each wheel's condition, typically used in lighter rail systems. In contrast, double wheel sensors monitor pairs of wheels, offering a broader perspective for heavier loads and ensuring stability. Both types enhance safety, improve maintenance schedules, and optimize rail operations.</p></p>
