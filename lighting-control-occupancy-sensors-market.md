<p><p>The lighting control occupancy sensors market includes various types that enhance energy efficiency. Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors detect motion through changes in infrared radiation, ideal for detecting human presence. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect occupancy, offering better coverage in complex spaces. Dual-technology sensors combine PIR and ultrasonic technologies, ensuring higher accuracy and reducing false triggers. Additional types may include microwave sensors or ambient light sensors, catering to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
