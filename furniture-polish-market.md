Solvent-based polishes typically contain oils that provide a deep shine and durable finish but may emit strong odors. Liquid polishes are easy to apply with a soft cloth, offering a quick cleaning and shine without complicated application methods. Aerosol polishes deliver an even mist, facilitating quick coverage and drying. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for convenience, effectiveness, and application ease.</p></p>
