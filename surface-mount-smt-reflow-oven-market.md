<p><strong>The Surface Mount SMT Reflow Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Mount Technology (SMT) reflow oven market comprises primarily two types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens use heated air to uniformly distribute heat across the circuit board, effectively melting solder paste for component attachment. In contrast, vapor phase ovens utilize boiling fluids to create a vapor environment, allowing for precise temperature control and reduced thermal stress on components. Both types cater to different production needs, offering efficiency and reliability in electronic assembly processes.</p></p>
