<p><p>The scuba diving fins market features two primary types: open heeled fins and closed heeled (full foot) fins. Open heeled fins are designed with a larger foot pocket, allowing divers to wear them over dive boots, providing added comfort and warmth in colder waters. They typically offer adjustable straps for a secure fit. Closed heeled fins, on the other hand, have a snug fit directly on the foot, offering better control and responsiveness, making them ideal for warm water diving.</p></p>
