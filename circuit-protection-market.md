<p><ul><li>Overcurrent Protection</li><li>ESD Protection</li><li>Overvoltage Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circuit protection market encompasses various types to safeguard electronic systems from damage. Overcurrent protection prevents excessive current flow, employing devices like fuses and circuit breakers. ESD protection mitigates damage from electrostatic discharge events, using components such as TVS diodes. Overvoltage protection safeguards circuits from voltage spikes, deploying surge protectors and varistors. Together, these protection types ensure the reliability and longevity of electronic devices by preventing failures caused by electrical anomalies.</p></p>
