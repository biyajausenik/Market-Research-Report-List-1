<p><p>PCB mounted power supplies are categorized into two main types: adjustable and fixed. Adjustable power supplies allow users to modify the output voltage or current based on specific requirements, making them ideal for applications needing flexibility. In contrast, fixed power supplies provide a predetermined output voltage and are designed for consistent performance in applications where precise regulation is essential. Both types serve diverse sectors, including consumer electronics, industrial machinery, and telecommunications, catering to varying energy needs and efficiency standards.</p></p>
