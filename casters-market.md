<p><p>The casters market is primarily divided into two types: rigid casters and swivel casters. Rigid casters have fixed wheels that move in a straight line, providing stability and support for heavy loads. They are ideal for applications requiring directional control. In contrast, swivel casters feature a pivoting wheel design, allowing for more maneuverability and flexibility in navigating tight spaces. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare, catering to specific mobility and load-bearing needs.</p></p>
