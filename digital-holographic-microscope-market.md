<p><p>The Digital Holographic Microscope market comprises two primary types: Reflection Type and Ordinary Type. The Reflection Type utilizes a reflective surface to capture holographic images, enabling enhanced observation of samples with varying refractive indices. In contrast, the Ordinary Type relies on traditional transmission techniques, producing detailed holograms through direct light interference. Both types offer distinct advantages in imaging and analysis, catering to diverse applications in fields such as biology, material science, and nanotechnology, thus driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
