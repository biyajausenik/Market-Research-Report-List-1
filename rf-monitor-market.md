<p><p>The RF monitor market comprises two main types: Area Safety Monitors and Personal Monitors. Area Safety Monitors are designed to continuously assess electromagnetic field levels in specific locations, ensuring that environments adhere to safety standards to protect workers and the public. In contrast, Personal Monitors are portable devices worn by individuals, providing real-time feedback on their exposure to RF radiation. Together, these monitors promote health and safety by enabling proactive measures against potential RF hazards in various settings.</p></p>
