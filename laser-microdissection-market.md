<p><p>The Laser Microdissection (LMD) market is categorized into Ultraviolet (UV) LMD and Infrared (IR) LMD types. UV LMD utilizes short-wavelength laser light to selectively target and isolate specific cells or tissue samples, providing high precision for analyzing minute structures. In contrast, IR LMD employs longer-wavelength lasers to cut through tissues with less thermal damage, making it suitable for a broader range of applications. Both technologies cater to diverse research needs, enhancing studies in genomics, proteomics, and pathology.</p></p>
