<p><p>The Modified Corn Starch market comprises several types, including Oxidized Corn Starch, Acid-modified Corn Starch, and Cationic Wet End Corn Starch. Oxidized Corn Starch enhances water solubility and thickening properties, making it ideal for sauces and dressings. Acid-modified Corn Starch offers improved clarity and stability, often used in confectionery applications. Cationic Wet End Corn Starch is designed for paper manufacturing, enhancing adhesion and retention during the wet end process, contributing to paper quality and performance.</p></p>
