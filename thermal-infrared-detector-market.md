<p><strong>The Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal infrared detector market is segmented into active and passive types. Active thermal infrared detectors require an external source of energy, such as a laser or light source, to detect thermal radiation, making them suitable for specific applications that demand high sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, passive thermal infrared detectors operate without external energy sources, detecting emitted infrared radiation naturally present in the environment. Passive detectors are commonly used in surveillance and thermal imaging, due to their simplicity and efficiency.</p></p>
