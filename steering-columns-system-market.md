<p><p>The steering columns system market is categorized into three main types: non-adjustable, manually adjustable, and electrically adjustable steering columns. Manually adjustable columns allow drivers to change the steering wheel’s position through a mechanical mechanism, enhancing comfort and ergonomics. Electrically adjustable steering columns offer advanced customization with electronic controls, allowing for precise adjustments at the touch of a button, improving driver convenience and overall driving experience. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
