<p><p>Microchannel coolers for electronic devices come in two primary types: stackable and array. Stackable microchannel coolers are designed to be stacked vertically, allowing for compact integration in systems where space is limited. They enhance cooling efficiency by increasing surface area while maintaining a small footprint. Array-type coolers consist of multiple channels organized in a grid formation, maximizing cooling performance across larger surfaces. Both types effectively manage heat in electronics, improving reliability and performance in advanced applications.</p></p>
