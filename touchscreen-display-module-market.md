<p><ul><li>Capacitive Touchscreen Display Module</li><li>Resistive Touchscreen Display Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touchscreen display module market is primarily divided into two types: capacitive and resistive touchscreen display modules. Capacitive modules utilize electrical properties of the human body, offering high sensitivity, multi-touch support, and clarity, making them ideal for smartphones and tablets. In contrast, resistive modules rely on pressure applied to the screen, enabling use with any object and providing better performance in harsh environments, making them suitable for industrial and medical applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
