<p><p>Water-based pigment ink for textiles is categorized into two main types: concentrated ink and non-concentrated ink. Concentrated ink contains a higher pigment content, allowing for vibrant colors and potentially requiring dilution before use. This type is ideal for achieving intense shades and detailed designs. Non-concentrated ink, on the other hand, comes ready to use and is more user-friendly, making it suitable for quick applications and smaller projects. Both types cater to diverse printing needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
