<p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) tanks market encompasses three primary types: metal CNG tanks, glass fiber CNG tanks, and carbon fiber CNG tanks. Metal tanks, typically made from steel or aluminum, offer durability and cost-effectiveness but weigh more. Glass fiber tanks combine lightweight construction with good resistance to pressure but are less common. Carbon fiber tanks are the lightest and most efficient, providing high strength-to-weight ratios, ideal for applications needing reduced weight and enhanced performance.</p></p>
