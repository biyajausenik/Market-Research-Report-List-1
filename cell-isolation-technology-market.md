<p><p>Cell isolation technology encompasses various methods for separating specific cell types from a heterogeneous mixture. Centrifugation utilizes differential density to separate cells based on size and mass, effectively isolating target cell populations. Flow cytometry employs fluorescently labeled antibodies and laser technology to analyze and sort cells in real-time, allowing for high-throughput isolation. Cell electrophoresis uses electric fields to segregate cells based on their charge and size, facilitating targeted extraction for further analysis in research and clinical applications.</p></p>
