<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Type</li><li>Pressure Sensitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cover tape in the semiconductor market is primarily classified into two types: heat-activated and pressure sensitive. Heat-activated cover tape bonds with the substrate upon exposure to heat, creating a secure seal suitable for applications requiring strong adhesion. In contrast, pressure-sensitive cover tape adheres upon contact, providing convenience and ease of application without the need for additional heat. Both types are crucial for protecting semiconductor components during packaging and transportation, ensuring reliability and functionality in various electronic applications.</p></p>
