<p><p>Normal-speed quenching oils are primarily categorized into two types: straight mineral oil and compounded type. Straight mineral oil is derived from refined crude oil, providing effective cooling and quenching properties. It is commonly used for heat treatment processes where minimal oxidation is needed. Compounded type quenching oils, on the other hand, contain additives that enhance performance, providing improved thermal stability, decreased flammability, and better resistance to oxidation. Both types serve critical roles in the metalworking industry, ensuring optimal cooling during heat treatment.</p></p>
