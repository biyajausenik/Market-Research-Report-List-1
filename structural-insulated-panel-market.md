<p><strong>The Structural Insulated Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) Panels</li><li>Rigid Polyurethane (PUR) Panels</li><li>Rigid Polyisocyanurate (PIR) Panels</li><li>Glass Wool Panels</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Structural Insulated Panel (SIP) market includes several types based on insulation materials. EPS panels utilize expanded polystyrene, offering cost-effective insulation and lightweight properties. Rigid polyurethane (PUR) panels provide superior thermal insulation and energy efficiency. Rigid polyisocyanurate (PIR) panels are similar to PUR but have enhanced fire resistance and better thermal performance. Glass wool panels, made from spun glass fibers, are known for acoustic insulation and lightweight benefits. Additionally, other materials may include combinations for specialized applications in construction.</p></p>
