<p><p>The digital drum set market comprises various types of drumheads, each offering distinct playing experiences. Rubber heads are durable and provide a firm surface, suitable for beginners. Mylar heads deliver a more acoustic-like feel but can be less responsive. Mesh heads are popular for their quiet operation and realistic rebound, mimicking traditional drum feel, making them ideal for practice. Silicone heads offer enhanced sensitivity and a softer touch, catering to drummers seeking a nuanced response and comfort during extended play.</p></p>
