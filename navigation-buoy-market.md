<p><p>The navigation buoy market comprises various types, including pillar buoys, can buoys, conical buoys, and others. Pillar buoys are tall structures that provide visibility over water, often used in deep waters. Can buoys have a cylindrical shape, typically marking channels. Conical buoys feature a pointed top, signaling safe passage in shallower waters. Other types include special-purpose buoys like mooring or lighted buoys, which serve specific navigational needs. Each type plays a crucial role in maritime safety and navigation.</p></p>
