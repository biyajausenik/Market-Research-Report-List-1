<p><strong>The Liquid Metal Electromagnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-phase Electromagnetic Induction Pump</li><li>Three-phase Electromagnetic Induction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid metal electromagnetic pump market is primarily classified into two types: single-phase electromagnetic induction pumps and three-phase electromagnetic induction pumps. Single-phase pumps are designed to handle a single flow of liquid metal, suitable for applications requiring lower flow rates and simpler operations. In contrast, three-phase pumps utilize a three-phase electrical system, enabling higher flow rates and enhanced efficiency, making them ideal for larger industrial applications and demanding conditions. Both types play crucial roles in effective metal processing and transportation.</p></p>
