<p><strong>The Power Distribution Unit (PDU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard PDU</li><li>Metrological PDU</li><li>Monitoring PDU</li><li>Switched PDU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Distribution Units (PDUs) are essential for managing power in data centers and IT environments. The Standard PDU distributes power to multiple devices without monitoring capabilities. Metrological PDUs measure and report power consumption, enabling efficient energy management. Monitoring PDUs offer real-time monitoring of power usage, ensuring optimal performance. Switched PDUs allow remote control of individual outlets, facilitating load management and reducing downtime. Together, these PDUs enhance power management, optimize efficiency, and improve operational reliability in critical systems.</p></p>
