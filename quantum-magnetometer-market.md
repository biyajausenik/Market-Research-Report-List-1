<p><strong>The Quantum Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optically-pumped Magnetometers</li><li>Solid-state Quantum Magnetometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantum magnetometer market is primarily divided into two types: optically-pumped magnetometers and solid-state quantum magnetometers. Optically-pumped magnetometers utilize the principles of atomic physics, relying on light to excite atoms and measure magnetic fields, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Solid-state quantum magnetometers, on the other hand, leverage defects in solid-state materials, like diamonds, to detect magnetic fields at the nanoscale, enabling applications in various fields, including healthcare, exploration, and materials science. Both types cater to distinct operational needs and environments.</p></p>
