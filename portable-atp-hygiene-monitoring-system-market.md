<p><p>The Portable ATP Hygiene Monitoring System market encompasses two primary types: Surface ATP Tests and Water ATP Tests. Surface ATP Tests are designed to detect adenosine triphosphate (ATP) residues on surfaces, helping assess hygiene levels in environments such as food processing and healthcare facilities. Water ATP Tests, on the other hand, analyze water samples for ATP to evaluate water quality and sanitation in various settings, including aquaculture and drinking water systems. Both test types ensure effective hygiene management and compliance with health standards.</p></p>
