<p><p>The Port wine market is primarily divided into two types: Vintage Port and Non-Vintage Port. Vintage Port is made from grapes of a single exceptional harvest and is bottled after a short aging period, developing complex flavors over time. Non-Vintage Port, on the other hand, is a blend of wines from multiple years, providing consistency and immediate drinkability. While Vintage Ports are often sought after for aging and investment, Non-Vintage Ports appeal to everyday consumers seeking quality at a more accessible price.</p></p>
