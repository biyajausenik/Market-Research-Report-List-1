<p><strong>The Consumer 3D Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>iToF</li><li>dToF</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Consumer 3D Vision Sensor market encompasses various technologies for depth perception and dimensional analysis. Structured Light sensors project patterns onto objects and analyze distortions to gauge depth. Intelligent Time-of-Flight (iToF) uses modulated light to measure time intervals for depth determination. Direct Time-of-Flight (dToF) employs a pulsed light source, accurately capturing distances by measuring the time it takes for light to return. Other methods may include laser scanning and stereo vision, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics, gaming, and augmented reality.</p></p>
