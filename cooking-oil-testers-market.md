<p><strong>The Cooking Oil Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooking oil testers can be categorized into two main market types: contact and contactless. Contact testers involve direct interaction with the oil, utilizing probes or sensors that measure specific parameters like acidity or temperature, requiring the device to physically touch the oil. In contrast, contactless testers utilize optical or electromagnetic techniques to analyze oil quality without direct contact, offering a more hygienic and non-intrusive testing method. Both types cater to food safety and quality assurance in culinary applications.</p></p>
