<p><p>The smart stethoscope market is divided into two primary types: wireless stethoscopes and wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or other wireless technologies to transmit acoustic sounds to compatible devices, enhancing mobility and convenience for healthcare professionals. In contrast, wired stethoscopes connect directly to devices via cables, often ensuring stable audio quality. Both types enable advanced features such as sound recording, analysis, and integration with telemedicine platforms, improving diagnostics and patient monitoring capabilities in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
