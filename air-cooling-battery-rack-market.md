<p><ul><li>Passive Air cCooling</li><li>Active Air Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air cooling battery rack market comprises two primary types: passive air cooling and active air cooling. Passive air cooling relies on natural airflow to dissipate heat from battery systems, typically utilizing ambient temperature differences and strategic rack designs for efficient heat management. In contrast, active air cooling employs mechanical systems, such as fans or blowers, to force air over the batteries, ensuring enhanced thermal regulation and improved performance. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal battery efficiency and lifespan in energy storage applications.</p></p>
