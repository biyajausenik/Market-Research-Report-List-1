<p><p>The pharmaceutical vial rubber stoppers market is segmented into two main types: conventional stoppers and film-coated stoppers. Conventional stoppers are made from elastomeric materials, offering reliable sealing for various pharmaceuticals. They are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and proven performance. In contrast, film-coated stoppers feature an additional polymer layer that provides enhanced protection against contamination and improves compatibility with sensitive formulations. This innovation helps maintain product integrity, making film-coated stoppers increasingly popular in modern pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
