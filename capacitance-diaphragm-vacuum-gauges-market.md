<p><p>Capacitance diaphragm vacuum gauges are precision instruments used to measure vacuum levels in various applications. The market is segmented into two primary types: ambient temperature capacitance manometers and heated capacitance manometers. Ambient temperature capacitance manometers operate effectively at standard conditions, making them suitable for moderate vacuum measurements. In contrast, heated capacitance manometers are designed to handle more extreme conditions by minimizing the effects of condensation and maximizing accuracy in low-pressure environments, thereby catering to specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
