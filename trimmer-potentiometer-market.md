<p><p>The trimmer potentiometer market is divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn. Single-turn trimmer potentiometers allow for a full rotation of the adjustment mechanism within one complete turn, making them suitable for applications requiring quick and simple adjustments. In contrast, multi-turn trimmer potentiometers enable multiple rotations, allowing for finer adjustments and greater precision. These multi-turn variants are often used in applications where accurate calibration is crucial, such as in instrumentation and sensitive electronic devices.</p></p>
