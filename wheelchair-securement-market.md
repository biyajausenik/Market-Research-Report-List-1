<p><ul><li>Non-Retractable</li><li>Retractable</li><li>Docking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair securement market is categorized into three main types: non-retractable, retractable, and docking systems. Non-retractable systems use fixed straps or ties to secure wheelchairs, offering simplicity but less convenience. Retractable systems feature automatic reels that extend and retract, providing ease of use and organization. Docking systems involve a designated spot where the wheelchair can be securely locked in place, ensuring enhanced safety during transportation. Each type addresses varied user needs and vehicle designs for effective wheelchair securement.</p></p>
