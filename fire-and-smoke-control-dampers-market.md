<p><p>Fire and smoke control dampers are essential components in HVAC systems designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke within buildings. Manual control dampers require manual adjustment to open or close, providing basic control but limited automation. In contrast, motorized control dampers offer automated functionality, using electric or pneumatic actuators for precise regulation based on fire detection systems or environmental conditions. These dampers enhance safety, ensure compliance with building codes, and optimize airflow in emergency situations.</p></p>
