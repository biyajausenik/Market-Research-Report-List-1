<p><p>The pool cleaners market encompasses various types, including robotic, suction, and pressure cleaners. Suction pool cleaners rely on the pool's filtration system to remove debris, making them simple and cost-effective. Pressure pool cleaners utilize water pressure to dislodge dirt and actively sweep the pool surface.
