<p><ul><li>Through-Hole</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Crystal and Oscillators market is segmented into two primary types: Through-Hole and Surface Mount. Through-Hole components involve pins inserted into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, offering robust physical connections suited for high-reliability applications in medical devices. In contrast, Surface Mount components are soldered directly onto the board's surface, allowing for compact designs and automated manufacturing processes. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring precise timing and frequency stability in various medical applications, enhancing device performance and reliability.</p></p>
