<p><p>The sipping tequila market is primarily categorized into two types: Pure Tequila and Mixto Tequila. Pure Tequila, also known as 100% agave tequila, is made exclusively from blue agave and offers a rich, authentic flavor profile. It is typically more expensive and sought after by connoisseurs. Mixto Tequila, on the other hand, comprises at least 51% agave, with the rest made up of other sugars. While more affordable, it often has a less refined taste, appealing to a broader audience.</p></p>
