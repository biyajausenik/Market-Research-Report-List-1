<p><p>The Professional Service Mobile Robots market includes Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs). AMRs are designed to navigate complex environments dynamically using advanced sensors and AI, enabling them to perform various tasks, such as delivery and inspection. In contrast, AGVs follow predetermined paths, typically employing magnetic strips or guide wires for navigation. While AMRs offer flexibility and adaptability, AGVs excel in repetitive, structured tasks, making both essential in industries like logistics, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
